The Octopus

by: Luke van der Woerd

The octopus is a sea dwelling cephalopod with 8 arms. In each of its 8 arms it contains a brain, which allows each arm to move independently from each other. These creatures also happen to have 3 hearts and blue blood. Adult octopuses feed on crabs, clams, snails, small fishes, and even other octopuses. Every species of octopus carry venom with varying levels of toxicity which they have the ability to inject using their beaks.
